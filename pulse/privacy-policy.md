# Dividend Tracker: Pulse - Privacy Policy

_Last updated: December 12, 2025_

Dividend Tracker: Pulse respects your privacy. This app is designed to function entirely on your device without collecting or transmitting personal data.

---

## 1. Data Collection

Dividend Tracker: Pulse **does not collect personal data**.

We do not collect, store, transmit, or process:
- names
- email addresses
- account identifiers
- financial account information
- location data
- device identifiers
- usage analytics

All portfolio data, dividend information, goals, assumptions, and settings are entered manually by the user.

---

## 2. Data Storage

All data is stored **locally on your device only** using Apple’s Core Data framework.

- No backend servers are used
- No cloud storage is used
- No account registration is required
- The developer has no access to your data

Deleting the app permanently removes all stored data.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

Dividend Tracker: Pulse does **not** integrate with third-party services that receive user data, including:
- analytics platforms
- advertising networks
- tracking SDKs
- data brokers

Apple may collect anonymized diagnostic or usage data according to its own privacy policy when you download or use the app.

---

## 4. Tracking

Dividend Tracker: Pulse does **not** track users.

- No cookies
- No advertising identifiers
- No fingerprinting
- No cross-app tracking

---

## 5. Financial Information Disclaimer

Dividend Tracker: Pulse does not connect to brokerages, banks, or financial institutions.

All financial figures, projections, and calculations are based solely on user-entered data and assumptions. Accuracy depends entirely on the information provided by the user.

---

## 6. User Rights

Because no personal data is collected or stored by the developer:
- there is no data to access, export, or correct
- there is no data held externally
- deleting the app removes all stored information

If privacy laws grant you additional rights, they are effectively satisfied by the app’s data-minimal design.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

Dividend Tracker: Pulse is not intended for children under the age of 13. No data is knowingly collected from children.

---

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
